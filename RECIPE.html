.. Mom Almond London Recipe ..

I believe everyone knows what is kuih almond london. So today, I feel like I want to share my mom almond london recipe. Every year when raya, almond london are one of the kuih raya that is a must have at our house.So the recipe is below.

The ingredients :

  • 1 butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 cup of flour
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tea spoon of vanilla essence
  • 1 cup of corn flour
  • 250g almond (bake half cooked)
  • almond nib (has been fried)
  • 250g cooking chocolate

Way to make it :

  1. Mix the butter, sugar, flour, egg yolk, vanilla essence and corn flour until it becomes dough.
  2. Coat almond with dough into oval shape.
  3. Bake the dough that has been shape for 20 minutes at 180 degree.
  4. Heat the cooking chocolate using the double boil method.
  5. Coating the biscuits that have been bake with the chocolate that have been melted and put it in the paper cup.
  6. Sprinkle the almond nib on the almond london that we have made.
  7. Let the chocolate harden before put it in any container.
